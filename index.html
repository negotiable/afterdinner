Welcome

Welcome to my mints page! A mint is a food item often consumed as an after-meal refreshment or before business and social engagements to improve breath odor. Unlike most types of food, mints are meant to be kept in one's mouth and slowly dissolved with saliva instead of being chewed and swallowed.

About this page

This website will host documentation of me trying out mints, with a focus on their dissolving rate in my mouth. I will be timing how long it takes each mint to dissolve in my mouth, and use video as proof of their dissolving. I will try to include some info about each mint I try, and share photos of the mints in and outside of their original packaging. This page will be where you can find all of this in one place. I chose the name "after dinner" for the page to reference a common time one might choose to dissolve a mint.
